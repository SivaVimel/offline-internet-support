Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the app take a few seconds to load?

The app initializes its local knowledge system when opening. This allows searches and explanations to work even without an internet connection.

Do I need internet to use the app?

No. The app works offline. When internet is available, it can also display web results and open websites privately.

Does the app collect personal data?

No. Offline Internet does not require accounts and does not collect personal data.
